ReTraceQA is a framework for evaluating the quality of reasoning traces produced by small language models in commonsense question answering tasks. The system provides comprehensive evaluation metrics and benchmarking capabilities.

**Publication:** ArXiv Preprint (October 2024)

**Paper:** [https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.09351](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.09351)
